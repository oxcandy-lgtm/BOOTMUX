## Current boundary

All named SAI mechanisms remain research concepts unless a later evidence record explicitly promotes one through specification, runnable implementation, tests, benchmarks, and declared support.

The current Build Week product does **not** claim a complete:

- automated terminal-event classifier;
- deterministic policy gate;
- structured recovery executor;
- recovery-capsule runtime;
- arbitrary-effect sandbox;
- autonomous diagnostic or repair system;
- measured superiority of the research transports.

The original blueprint is preserved because it contains BOOTMUX's full experimental direction and SAI-originated synthesis. Statements inside that original file describing policy-gated recovery or later stages are design goals, not evidence that those components exist in the current product.
